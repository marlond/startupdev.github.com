What is Startup DEV Rumble?

Startup DEV Rumble is a challenge for developers and designers. You must gather a team of up to 4 people and will have 48h to deliver an web application from scratch.

The app can be developed in any programming language or web framework.

The apps will then be rated by the public on Design, Innovation, Usefulness and Beauty.

Check the rules and the FAQ if you need more information.
